👋 Meet the Experts

These AI-powered reviewers are trained on WhatsApp's internal guidelines and best practices.

🛡️

PXFN

Legal + Policy + Risk

Reviews content for policy violations, legal concerns, and risk issues. Trained on WhatsApp's content policies, GDPR/CCPA compliance guidelines, and Meta's advertising policies.

Looks for:
  • Misleading or promotional language
  • Unverified claims ("guaranteed", "100%")
  • Privacy and data handling accuracy
  • Regulatory compliance issues
🎨

Designer

Product Designer

Evaluates visual hierarchy, layout, and design system compliance. Trained on the WhatsApp Design System (WDS) patterns and component guidelines.

Looks for:
  • Information hierarchy and visual flow
  • WDS pattern consistency
  • Typography and spacing issues
  • Button/CTA placement
📊

PM

Product Manager

Reviews content for product alignment and user value. Trained on product strategy frameworks and WhatsApp's approach to feature development.

Looks for:
  • Clear value proposition
  • Logical user flow
  • Feature completeness
  • Edge cases and error states
📣

GTM

Go-to-Market / PMM

Evaluates messaging for market readiness and global reach. Trained on WhatsApp brand guidelines, localization best practices, and market-specific considerations.

Looks for:
  • Brand voice consistency
  • Localization readiness (30% text expansion)
  • Call-to-action effectiveness
  • Cultural sensitivity
🔬

UXR

UX Researcher

Reviews from a user comprehension perspective. Trained on UX research methodologies and common usability patterns from WhatsApp user studies.

Looks for:
  • User comprehension issues
  • Cognitive load problems
  • Mental model mismatches
  • Decision-making friction
✍️

Content

Content Designer

Reviews for quality, clarity, and voice consistency. Trained on WhatsApp's voice and tone guidelines, UX writing best practices, and localization constraints.

Looks for:
  • Grammar, spelling, punctuation
  • WhatsApp voice (simple, human, clear)
  • Conciseness and clarity
  • String length constraints
